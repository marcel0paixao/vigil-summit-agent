# Product Roadmap

## Current

- Registration, consent, enrichment, qualification, cadences, bidirectional engagement, meetings,
  funnel analytics, privacy operations, and auditable decisions.
- Synthetic providers for reproducible local operation.
- Configurable Apollo, Resend, and Claude integrations.

## Next

- Provider-specific inbound retrieval and calendar webhook adapters.
- Workspace quotas and backpressure dashboards.
- Accessibility audit and browser-level end-to-end coverage.
- Load validation for regional event programs.
- Optional WhatsApp adapter with channel-specific consent and templates.
